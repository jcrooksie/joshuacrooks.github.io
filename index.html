About me

I'm Josh, a nature photographer based in Nova Scotia, Canada. Since 2017, I have been hiking and capturing the beautiful views of nature throuhgout my home province of Nova Scotia, which can involve the awesome landscapes and famous landmarks Nova Scotia has to offer, or the rare and sacred wildife that can be found here in the maritimes.

You can check ouy my work and experiences below. Photography for me is a passion more then a profession.

What type of work do you expect to see?

  • I focus mainly on landscape and wildlife. Doing photography as a hobby doesn't always let me travel to famous and exotic places often so, I take photos of everyday things when living in Nova Scotia, and try and make awesome photos out of them.
